Throwback Problem created back in 2014.

A circle passes through points A,C and E, with point F being the centre of the circle.
AB is tangential to the circle.
AB is parallel to CD.
Points A, E and D are collinear.
Points B, E, F and C are collinear.
Given length AB = diameter CE = d, find the area of the trapezium in terms of d.
